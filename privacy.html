    Privacy Policy

    Your Privacy

    Last updated: June 2026

    This policy explains how personal information submitted through this website is collected, held, and used. The website is operated by Nicholas Girling, an independent artist based in Melbourne, Australia.

    Information collected

    The contact form on this site is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit an enquiry, Formspree collects the information you provide: typically your name, email address, and message. Formspree may also collect technical data such as your IP address and browser details in accordance with their own privacy policy.

    No other personal data is collected by this website. There are no tracking cookies, no analytics scripts, and no advertising pixels.

    How your information is used

    Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. It is not shared with third parties, sold, or used for unsolicited marketing without your consent.

    If you express interest in being added to the mailing list for new releases, your email address will be retained for that purpose. You may unsubscribe at any time by replying to any mailing list communication or by sending a request directly to Nicholas Girling.

    Mailing list

    Joining the mailing list is optional and requires your active agreement. The list is used to announce new original paintings, limited edition print releases, and occasional studio news. It is not used for promotional offers from third parties.

    To opt in, simply indicate in your enquiry message that you would like to be added. To opt out at any time, reply to any email from the list with the word "unsubscribe" or contact Nicholas Girling directly.

    Third-party services

    This website uses Formspree to process contact form submissions. Formspree operates its own servers and privacy policy, which governs how they handle any data that passes through their service. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's servers; your browser may send standard request data to Google when loading the page typefaces.

    No other third-party services are used on this website.

    Data retention

    Enquiry messages are retained only as long as necessary to address the relevant conversation. Mailing list details are retained until you request removal. You may request deletion of your information at any time and it will be removed promptly.

    Copyright

    Contact

    For any questions about this policy, or to request removal of your personal information, please use the contact form on the home page or write directly to Nicholas Girling via the enquiry form.
