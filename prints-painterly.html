    Prints · River Series

    Fine art prints. Printed on archival cotton-rag. Limited Editions signed and numbered.

    Whlst the original River Series paintings are sold out, full size signed art prints and commissions in the theme are available. 

    The artwork began as acrylic on linen canvas: marks were made with brushes, palette knives and hands. Those painted surfaces were then translated by the artist himself to become fine art prints and are giclee printed on thick archival cotton-rag paper with long life pigment inks.

    Each print is available in two size options to help match your space. Prints on paper come with a white edge where I sign the artwork.
