Behind the Work

Studio Process

I

Concept & Sketching

Every piece begins with preliminary sketches and colour studies, exploring the emotional core of the work.

II

Layering & Development

Through multiple layers of paint and texture, the work evolves organically, allowing for unexpected discoveries.

III

Refinement & Completion

Final adjustments are made with careful attention to composition, colour harmony, and emotional impact.

IV

Preparation & Delivery

Each piece is professionally varnished, framed if requested, and carefully prepared for shipping or collection.
