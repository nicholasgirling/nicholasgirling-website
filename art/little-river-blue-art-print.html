A quiet study of light moving across water. Working in thin, transparent layers of acrylic, this piece captures the ephemeral quality of afternoon light on the river surface — a subject returned to repeatedly for its capacity to hold both stillness and movement simultaneously. The palette moves between warm ochre and cool grey, reflecting the shifting sky.
